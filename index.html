The product — Eden in 10 advantages

Every buyer fear on Bang Tao — and how Eden closes it. This is the message platform all the content is built on.

Dense construction, greenery squeezed between buildings

70% of the land is a park — 12 ha, low-density

A courtyard that belongs to cars

Car-free: cars go underground, gardens above

Shared lobbies and long corridors

Elevator from the parking straight to your floor

Standard 2.7 m ceilings

3.2 m ceilings — air from the doorway

Hearing the neighbours upstairs

Triple glazing + thick slabs — engineered silence

One lobby bar for the whole complex

Six restaurants of its own — resort-level dining

One standard pool in the yard

Infinity pool, sand-entry lagoon, 150 m pool

A gym in the basement

Flagship wellness centre and clubhouse

Nowhere to take the kids

A kids' club building, school nearby, Boat Avenue in 5 min

Crowds and walk-through grounds

50 steps to the sea, gated grounds, privacy
