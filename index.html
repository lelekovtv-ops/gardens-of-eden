Concept I — “Grand Budapest on Bang Tao”

A legendary Thai concierge answers one question in two minutes — “What is Gardens of Eden?” — and leads us through the garden, the home, the kitchen and the island, helping with his own hands in every story.

  • Symmetry & frontal staging
  • Chapter title cards
  • Deadpan humour
  • Sage green palette
  • 60 frames scripted
  • Seamless loop ending

Film structure: Prologue → Garden → Home → project insert R1–R12 → Kitchen → Phuket → Finale (loops back to frame 1)

08

Brand film & showroom

The anchor of the whole system — one shoot, five deliverables.

  • Master 2:00 with sound — for the website and presentations
  • Lobby loop without sound — seamless, always on in the showroom
  • Project insert R1–R12 — renders of the development for pitches
  • Cutdowns 30 / 15 sec — for advertising
  • Verticals 9:16 from the chapters — for social media

Status: script of 60 frames done, previz in progress.

09

YouTube

A channel that builds demand for “life on Phuket” — and makes Gardens of Eden its answer.

  • Project overview films
  • Bang Tao neighbourhood guides
  • “Why Phuket” — investor explainers
  • Construction vlog until handover
  • Interviews with the team behind the project

Cadence: 2–4 videos per month.

10

Reels / Shorts

A vertical stream cut from the film shoots and the site — attention in the first second.

  • 8–16 reels per month
  • Hooks from the film world — the concierge, the details, the island
  • Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
  • RU / EN versions

11

VSL — video sales letter

A selling video for the landing page: it walks a cold visitor to a request.

  • Structure: dream → the project → the offer → call to action
  • 3–5 minutes, presenter-led or narrated
  • A/B versions of the opening
  • RU / EN / ZH localisations

12

Training videos for sellers

A closed video library so every seller and agent tells the project the same way.

  • The product by unit type — what to show and say
  • Objection handling — short answer clips
  • Onboarding for new agents
  • Broker & agency kit
